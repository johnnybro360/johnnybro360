Welcome to Bayside Boomers Netball Club

Bayside boomers consisted of 34 teams across all age groups for the season 2022 which is now complete.

Teams compete in the RAC Insurance League with an Open 19/u team plus Pilbara Netball Association's

Winter Roster where we field teams from Under 11 through to Open Premier League.

We are one of the largest clubs in Pilbara and couldn't be happier to have so many players being able

to play the sport of netball with us, an achievement we are very proud of.

Achieving the very best for our members, our Board and Committee can be found on the 'Contact Us' page
